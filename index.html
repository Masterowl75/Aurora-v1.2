New Mechanic!

  • Presenting the all new mechanic, Wavedashing!
  • With Wavedasing you can gain massive amounts of speed
  • To perform a Wavedash you must dash across the floor, and then jump mid dash to go ZOOM

Trouble In Paradise?

  • can't quite get it right?
  • Fun Fact: New mechanic means new skill
  • Press jump before you dash and your won't perform the wavedash
  • do it too late and you won't achieve the desired amount of distance
  • by timing your jump correctly you can maximise the distance that you boost from the wavedash
